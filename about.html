--- layout: single title: About permalink: / author_profile: true --- Welcome to the GitHub website of Adaptive Epigenomes. We are a newly established research group at the University of Helsinki interested in the role of epigenomics in ecology and evolution, Primarily using plants like birch trees (genus Betula), we ask questions such as: • Do epigenetic mechanisms like DNA methylation contribute to adaptive or maladaptive phenotypic plasticity? • How do evolutionary processes such as selection, drift, and gene flow influence epigenetic variation? • How do structural variants, especially transposable elements (TEs), impact the plasticity of the epigenome?
