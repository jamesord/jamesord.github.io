--- layout: single title: About permalink: / author_profile: true --- Welcome to the GitHub website of the Adaptive Epigenomes group. We are a newly established research group at the University of Helsinki interested in the role of epigenetics and related mechanisms in ecology and evolution. Primarily using plants like birch trees (genus Betula), we ask questions such as:
  • Do epigenetic mechanisms like DNA methylation contribute to adaptive or maladaptive phenotypic plasticity?
  • How do evolutionary processes such as selection, drift, and gene flow influence epigenetic variation?
  • How do genomic structural variants shape the epigenome and its plasticity?
